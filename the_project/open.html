Auray City


A FabLab High Performance Computing center


Algoric is an High Performance Computing center, located at Auray (France), and hosted by a FabLab: La Fabrique du Loch.

Our HPC center is not conventional, as efforts are made on what is around supercomputing, instead of aiming maximum computational power.

Flexible

Provide calculation ressources to all

Green

Recycling hardware, recycling heat

Gateway

Teaching supercomputing to access larger clusters

Open

Fully opensource based approach
